Our Mission

CraftConnect is dedicated to bridging the gap between rural artisans and urban markets. We aim to empower artisans by providing access to skill development, market opportunities, and fair pricing. Our platform fosters a community where traditional crafts thrive and artisans' livelihoods are enhanced.

Our Values

We believe in the power of collaboration, transparency, and sustainability. CraftConnect is committed to ethical practices, ensuring fair compensation for artisans, and promoting environmentally responsible production. We value the preservation of cultural heritage and the unique skills of each artisan.

Our Impact

Since our inception, CraftConnect has supported over 500 artisans, facilitated 20 skill training workshops, and connected artisans with buyers across the country. We have seen a significant increase in artisans' income and a renewed interest in traditional crafts. Join us in making a difference in the lives of rural artisans and preserving our cultural heritage.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, our platform was born from a passion for craftsmanship and the challenges faced by rural artisans in modern economies. From 25 Tamil Nadu artisans to over 2,500 today, we support heritage while creating sustainable futures.
