About Chinnadurai

Chinnadurai is a skilled potter from Madurai, Tamil Nadu, specializing in the traditional art of blue pottery and terracotta. With over 15 years of experience, Chinnadurai creates intricate designs inspired by nature and local culture. His work has been featured in several exhibitions and is highly sought after by collectors and enthusiasts alike.
